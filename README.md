# Clicked — AI Reading Assistant & Explainer

**Highlight anything → understand it.** Clicked is a browser extension for Chrome and Edge that explains any text you highlight — immediately, right where you're reading, on any webpage or PDF. No prompts, no chat windows, no AI sidebars.

🌐 **Website:** [tryclicked.app](https://tryclicked.app?ref=github) · 🧩 [**Chrome Web Store**](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/clicked/ocmgkhfoojblkdeaciadnnhklblaldpk) · 🧩 [**Edge Add-ons**](https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/detail/ffnfbkmnpmocooimnemhmlcjgkopeioc)

---

## How it works

1. **Highlight** a word, sentence, or paragraph on any page.
2. An explanation appears next to it — at three depths you tap through:
   - **Overview** — the ten-second version
   - **Detail** — the full picture
   - **Analogy** — a real-world example that makes it stick (tap ↺ for a fresh one anytime)
3. Keep reading. That's the whole workflow.

Works on webpages and PDFs, in **15+ languages** — read in any of them, get it explained in yours.

## What people use it for

- **Language learning** — read foreign-language news and books with in-context explanations in your own language, with pronunciation on tap. An AI dictionary that translates *meaning*, not just words.
- **Studying** — make sense of dense papers and contracts one paragraph at a time; get an "explain like I'm five" version; every saved term lands in your private library, exportable as flashcards for **Anki, Quizlet, and Vaia**.
- **Everyday reading** — finance jargon, legal clauses, medical terms, code snippets, internet slang (there's a **Slang mode** that explains things in a casual, internet-native voice).

## Privacy is a founding principle

- Clicked sees only the words you highlight — never the rest of the page, your browsing history, or your other tabs.
- Your saved library lives on your device. We never see or store it.
- Domain blocking built in: banking, email, and health sites are pre-blocked (gmail, major banks, brokerages, patient portals) and you can add any site.
- Bring-your-own-key plan: your API key is stored locally and never touches our servers.

Full policy: [tryclicked.app/privacy](https://tryclicked.app/privacy?ref=github)
